Vanilla Cupcakes

Prep: 20 min
Cook: 18 min
Makes: 12 cupcakes

Ingredients

For Cupcakes:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/3 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

For Buttercream Frosting:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Food coloring (optional)
  • Sprinkles for decoration

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375¬∞F (190¬∞C). Line 12-cup muffin tin with paper liners.
  2. In a large bowl, cream together softened butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Beat in egg and vanilla extract until well combined.
  4. In separate bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt.
  5. Alternately add dry ingredients and milk to butter mixture, beginning and ending with flour.
  6. Mix until just combined - don't overmix.
  7. Divide batter evenly among cupcake liners, filling each about 2/3 full.
  8. Bake for 18-20 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
  9. Cool in pan for 5 minutes, then transfer to wire rack to cool completely.
  10. For frosting: Beat butter until creamy, gradually add powdered sugar.
  11. Add milk and vanilla, beat until smooth and pipeable consistency.
  12. Pipe or spread frosting on cooled cupcakes and add sprinkles.

Chef's Tips

  • Room temperature ingredients mix better for fluffy cupcakes
  • Don't overmix batter to avoid dense, tough cupcakes
  • Use ice cream scoop for evenly sized cupcakes
  • Test doneness with toothpick - it should come out with few moist crumbs
  • Cool completely before frosting to prevent melting
  • Store frosted cupcakes covered at room temperature for 2 days
