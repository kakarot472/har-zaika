Orange Chicken

Prep: 20 min
Cook: 25 min
Serves: 4

Ingredients

For Chicken:

  • 1 lb chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • Oil for deep frying

For Orange Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup fresh orange juice
  • 1/4 cup orange zest
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp ginger, minced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch mixed with 3 tbsp water
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds

Instructions

  1. Mix cornstarch, flour, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  2. Dip chicken pieces in beaten egg, then coat with flour mixture.
  3. Heat oil to 350¬∞F (175¬∞C) and fry chicken pieces until golden brown and crispy (5-6 minutes).
  4. Remove chicken and drain on paper towels.
  5. For sauce, combine orange juice, orange zest, brown sugar, soy sauce, and rice vinegar in a pan.
  6. Add minced ginger, garlic, and red pepper flakes.
  7. Bring to a boil and simmer for 3-4 minutes.
  8. Add cornstarch slurry and stir until sauce thickens.
  9. Add fried chicken to the sauce and toss to coat evenly.
  10. Cook for 2-3 minutes until chicken is well glazed.
  11. Garnish with chopped green onions and sesame seeds.
  12. Serve hot with steamed rice.

Chef's Tips

  • Use fresh orange juice and zest for best flavor
  • Don't overcrowd the pan when frying chicken
  • Maintain oil temperature for crispy coating
  • Add cornstarch slurry gradually to avoid lumps
  • Toss chicken in sauce just before serving to maintain crispiness
  • Adjust sweetness and spice levels to taste
