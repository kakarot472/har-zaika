Mint Margarita

Prep: 10 min
Cook: 0 min
Serves: 2

Ingredients

For Mint Margarita:

  • 1 cup fresh mint leaves
  • 1/4 cup fresh lime juice
  • 2 tbsp sugar or simple syrup
  • 1 cup cold water
  • 1/2 cup ice cubes
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 tsp lemon juice

For Garnish:

  • Fresh mint sprigs
  • Lime wedges
  • Salt for rim (optional)
  • Extra ice cubes

Instructions

  1. Wash fresh mint leaves thoroughly and pat dry.
  2. In a blender, add mint leaves, lime juice, and sugar.
  3. Add a pinch of salt and lemon juice for extra flavor.
  4. Pour in cold water and blend on high speed for 30-45 seconds.
  5. Strain the mixture through a fine mesh to remove mint pieces (optional).
  6. Add ice cubes to the blended mixture and stir well.
  7. Taste and adjust sweetness or lime juice as needed.
  8. If desired, rim glasses with salt by rubbing lime wedge on rim and dipping in salt.
  9. Fill glasses with fresh ice cubes.
  10. Pour mint margarita over ice, leaving room at the top.
  11. Garnish with fresh mint sprigs and lime wedges.
  12. Serve immediately while cold and refreshing.

Chef's Tips

  • Use fresh mint leaves for the best flavor and aroma
  • Don't over-blend or the drink may become bitter
  • Adjust sweetness to taste - start with less sugar
  • Chill glasses beforehand for extra refreshing experience
  • Add sparkling water for a fizzy version
  • Best consumed immediately after preparation
