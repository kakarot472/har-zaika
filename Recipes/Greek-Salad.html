Greek Salad

Prep: 10 min
Cook: 0 min
Serves: 4

Ingredients

For Salad:


  • 4 large tomatoes, cut into wedges
  • 1 large cucumber, sliced
  • 1 medium red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 200g feta cheese, cubed
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives
  • 2 tbsp fresh oregano (or 1 tbsp dried)
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

For Dressing:

  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1/2 tsp lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Prepare all vegetables: Cut tomatoes into wedges, slice cucumber and red onion, and slice bell pepper.
  2. In a large salad bowl, combine tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and bell pepper.
  3. Add cubed feta cheese and Kalamata olives to the bowl.
  4. For the dressing, whisk together olive oil, red wine vinegar, minced garlic, oregano, salt, pepper, and lemon juice in a small bowl.
  5. Pour the dressing over the salad ingredients.
  6. Gently toss everything together to coat with dressing.
  7. Sprinkle fresh oregano over the salad.
  8. Let the salad sit for 10-15 minutes to allow flavors to meld.
  9. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.
  10. Serve immediately at room temperature or chilled.

Chef's Tips

  • Use ripe, firm tomatoes for the best flavor and texture
  • Don't skip the resting time - it helps flavors develop
  • Use authentic Greek feta cheese for traditional taste
  • Kalamata olives are essential for authentic Greek flavor
  • Extra virgin olive oil makes a significant difference in taste
  • Can be made ahead and stored in refrigerator for up to 2 days
