Bhindi Masala

Prep: 15 min
Cook: 30 min
Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 500g fresh okra (bhindi), washed and dried
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 3-4 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 3-4 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala powder
  • 1 tsp amchur (dry mango powder)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Wash okra thoroughly and pat completely dry. Trim ends and cut into 1-inch pieces.
  2. Heat oil in a heavy-bottomed pan over medium heat.
  3. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  4. Add sliced onions and saut√© until golden brown (8-10 minutes).
  5. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Cook for 2 minutes.
  6. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they break down and become pulpy.
  7. Add turmeric, red chili powder, and coriander powder. Mix well.
  8. Add the cut okra pieces and mix gently to coat with spices.
  9. Cover and cook on medium-low heat for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  10. Add salt, garam masala, and amchur powder. Mix gently.
  11. Cook uncovered for 5 more minutes until okra is tender and not slimy.
  12. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with roti or rice.

Chef's Tips

  • Ensure okra is completely dry before cutting to avoid sliminess
  • Don't add water while cooking - okra releases its own moisture
  • Cook on medium heat to prevent burning and ensure even cooking
  • Amchur (dry mango powder) adds tangy flavor and reduces sliminess
  • Stir gently to avoid breaking the okra pieces
  • Choose young, tender okra for best results
