Almond Cream Cake

Prep: 30 min
Cook: 35 min
Serves: 8-10

Ingredients

For Almond Cake:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tsp almond extract
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup sliced almonds

For Cream Frosting:

  • 2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp almond extract
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

For Decoration:

  • 1/2 cup toasted sliced almonds
  • Fresh berries (optional)
  • Powdered sugar for dusting

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350¬∞F (175¬∞C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. In a large bowl, cream together butter and sugar until light and fluffy (3-4 minutes).
  3. Beat in eggs one at a time, then add almond and vanilla extracts.
  4. In separate bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt.
  5. Alternately add dry ingredients and milk to butter mixture, beginning and ending with flour.
  6. Fold in sliced almonds gently.
  7. Divide batter evenly between prepared pans.
  8. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
  9. Cool in pans for 10 minutes, then turn out onto wire racks to cool completely.
  10. For frosting: Whip heavy cream until soft peaks form.
  11. Gradually add powdered sugar and extracts, whip until stiff peaks form.
  12. Place one cake layer on serving plate, spread with half the cream.
  13. Top with second layer and remaining cream.
  14. Decorate with toasted almonds and berries if desired.
  15. Dust with powdered sugar before serving. Refrigerate until ready to serve.

Chef's Tips

  • Room temperature ingredients mix better for lighter cake texture
  • Don't overmix batter to avoid dense cake
  • Toast almonds lightly for enhanced nutty flavor
  • Chill cream and bowl before whipping for better results
  • Store cake covered in refrigerator for up to 3 days
  • Let cake come to room temperature before serving for best taste
