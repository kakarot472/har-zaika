Boba Tea

Prep: 15 min
Cook: 30 min
Serves: 2

Ingredients

For Tapioca Pearls:

  • 1/2 cup dried tapioca pearls (black or white)
  • 4 cups water for boiling
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp water for syrup

For Tea Base:

  • 4 black tea bags or 2 tbsp loose black tea
  • 2 cups hot water
  • 1/4 cup sugar (adjust to taste)

For Assembly:

  • 1 cup whole milk or non-dairy milk
  • Ice cubes
  • Wide straws for serving

Instructions

  1. Bring 4 cups of water to a rolling boil in a large pot.
  2. Add tapioca pearls and stir gently to prevent sticking.
  3. Cook for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until pearls are translucent.
  4. Turn off heat, cover, and let sit for 15 minutes to finish cooking.
  5. Meanwhile, make brown sugar syrup by heating 1/4 cup brown sugar with 2 tbsp water until dissolved.
  6. Drain cooked pearls and rinse with cold water.
  7. Mix pearls with brown sugar syrup and set aside.
  8. Brew strong black tea with hot water for 5 minutes.
  9. Remove tea bags and stir in sugar while tea is hot.
  10. Let tea cool to room temperature, then refrigerate until cold.
  11. To assemble: Add 2-3 tablespoons of sweetened pearls to each glass.
  12. Fill glasses with ice, pour in cold tea, and top with milk.
  13. Stir well and serve immediately with wide straws.

Chef's Tips

  • Don't overcook pearls or they'll become mushy
  • Cooked pearls are best consumed within 4 hours
  • Try different tea bases like green tea, oolong, or Thai tea
  • Adjust sweetness by varying sugar in tea and pearl syrup
  • For fruit flavors, add fresh fruit purees or syrups
  • Store leftover pearls in syrup to prevent hardening
