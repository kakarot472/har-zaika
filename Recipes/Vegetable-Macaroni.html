Vegetable Macaroni

Prep: 15 min
Cook: 25 min
Serves: 4-5

Ingredients

  • 2 cups macaroni pasta
  • 2 tbsp olive oil or cooking oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 cup green beans, chopped
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp red chili flakes
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese (optional)
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 cup vegetable broth or water

Instructions

  1. Cook macaroni pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a large pan or skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add chopped onions and saut√© until translucent (3-4 minutes).
  4. Add minced garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  5. Add diced bell pepper, carrot, and green beans. Cook for 5-6 minutes until vegetables start to soften.
  6. Add corn kernels and chopped tomatoes. Cook for 3-4 minutes.
  7. Stir in tomato paste, Italian seasoning, red chili flakes, black pepper, and salt.
  8. Add vegetable broth or water and let it simmer for 5 minutes.
  9. Add the cooked macaroni to the pan and toss everything together.
  10. Cook for 2-3 minutes until pasta is well coated with the sauce.
  11. If using cheese, sprinkle it over the pasta and let it melt.
  12. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot.

Chef's Tips

  • Don't overcook the pasta - it should be al dente for best texture
  • Cut all vegetables into similar sizes for even cooking
  • Add vegetables in order of cooking time - harder vegetables first
  • You can add any seasonal vegetables of your choice
  • For extra flavor, add a splash of white wine when cooking vegetables
  • Serve immediately while hot for best taste and texture
