Chicken Pizza

Prep: 30 min
Cook: 20 min
Serves: 4

Ingredients

For Pizza Dough:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

For Chicken Topping:


  • 2 chicken breasts, diced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For Assembly:

  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1/4 cup parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/2 red onion, sliced
  • 1/4 cup black olives, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions

  1. Mix yeast, sugar, and warm water. Let sit for 5 minutes until foamy.
  2. In a bowl, combine flour and salt. Add yeast mixture and olive oil.
  3. Knead dough for 8-10 minutes until smooth. Place in oiled bowl, cover, and rise for 1 hour.
  4. Meanwhile, season diced chicken with garlic powder, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  5. Heat olive oil in a pan and cook chicken until golden and cooked through. Set aside.
  6. Preheat oven to 475¬∞F (245¬∞C).
  7. Roll out dough on floured surface to fit your pizza pan.
  8. Transfer dough to greased pizza pan or baking sheet.
  9. Spread pizza sauce evenly over dough, leaving 1-inch border for crust.
  10. Sprinkle half the mozzarella cheese over sauce.
  11. Add cooked chicken, bell peppers, onions, and olives.
  12. Top with remaining mozzarella and parmesan cheese.
  13. Bake for 12-15 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  14. Garnish with fresh basil and red pepper flakes. Slice and serve hot.

Chef's Tips

  • Let dough rise in a warm place for best results
  • Pre-cook chicken to ensure it's fully cooked on pizza
  • Don't overload with toppings or crust will be soggy
  • Use a pizza stone for crispier crust if available
  • Let pizza cool for 2-3 minutes before slicing
  • Can use store-bought dough to save time
