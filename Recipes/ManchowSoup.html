Manchow Soup

Prep: 15 min
Cook: 20 min
Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable stock
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, cabbage, bell peppers), julienned
  • 1/4 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2-3 green chilies, chopped
  • 1/4 cup spring onions, chopped
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp chili sauce
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper powder
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch mixed with 3 tbsp water
  • 1 egg, beaten (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/4 cup cooked noodles
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Fried noodles for topping

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium-high heat.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Saut√© for 1 minute.
  3. Add julienned vegetables and mushrooms. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes.
  4. Add chicken or vegetable stock and bring to a boil.
  5. Add soy sauce, chili sauce, vinegar, and black pepper. Mix well.
  6. Add cooked noodles and simmer for 2-3 minutes.
  7. Mix cornstarch slurry and add to soup, stirring continuously until thickened.
  8. If using egg, slowly drizzle beaten egg while stirring to create egg ribbons.
  9. Add salt to taste and adjust seasonings.
  10. Add chopped spring onions and cook for 1 more minute.
  11. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  12. Serve hot topped with crispy fried noodles.

Chef's Tips

  • Cut vegetables into thin, uniform julienne strips for even cooking
  • Don't overcook vegetables - they should retain some crunch
  • Add cornstarch slurry gradually to avoid lumps
  • Adjust spice levels with chili sauce and black pepper
  • Serve immediately while hot for best taste
  • Top with crispy fried noodles just before serving
