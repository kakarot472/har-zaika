Mango Pancakes

Prep: 10 min
Cook: 15 min
Serves: 4

Ingredients

For Pancakes:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Butter for cooking

For Mango Topping:

  • 2 ripe mangoes, diced
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • Whipped cream (optional)
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish
  • Powdered sugar for dusting

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, combine milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla extract.
  3. Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Don't overmix.
  4. Let batter rest for 5 minutes while preparing mango topping.
  5. For topping, combine diced mangoes with honey and lime juice. Set aside.
  6. Heat a non-stick pan or griddle over medium heat. Add a little butter.
  7. Pour 1/4 cup batter for each pancake onto the hot pan.
  8. Cook until bubbles form on surface and edges look set (2-3 minutes).
  9. Flip and cook for another 1-2 minutes until golden brown.
  10. Repeat with remaining batter, adding butter as needed.
  11. Stack pancakes on serving plates and top with mango mixture.
  12. Drizzle with maple syrup, add whipped cream if desired, and garnish with mint.
  13. Dust with powdered sugar and serve immediately.

Chef's Tips

  • Don't overmix the batter - lumps are okay for fluffy pancakes
  • Let batter rest for better texture and rise
  • Use ripe but firm mangoes for best flavor and texture
  • Keep pancakes warm in a 200¬∞F oven if making large batches
  • Adjust heat to prevent burning - medium heat works best
  • Serve immediately for best taste and texture
