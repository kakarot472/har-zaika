Chinese Fried Rice

Prep: 15 min
Cook: 10 min
Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 3 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old)
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn)
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: cooked chicken, shrimp, or tofu

Instructions

  1. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a large wok or skillet over high heat.
  2. Add beaten eggs and scramble. Remove and set aside.
  3. Add remaining oil to the pan. Add garlic and cook for 30 seconds.
  4. Add mixed vegetables and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Add rice, breaking up any clumps. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes.
  6. Return eggs to pan. Add soy sauce and sesame oil.
  7. Stir everything together and cook for 1-2 minutes more.
  8. Garnish with green onions and serve hot.

Chef's Tips

  • Use day-old rice for best texture - fresh rice can become mushy
  • Keep the heat high for authentic wok flavor
  • Add protein of choice in step 4 with vegetables
