Infused Water

Prep: 5 min
Cook: 0 min
Serves: 2-3

Ingredients

Cucumber Mint Water:

  • 1 cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 10-12 fresh mint leaves
  • 4 cups water
  • Ice cubes

Lemon Ginger Water:

  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 1 inch fresh ginger, sliced
  • 4 cups water
  • Ice cubes

Berry Basil Water:

  • 1/2 cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries)
  • 6-8 fresh basil leaves
  • 4 cups water
  • Ice cubes

Orange Cinnamon Water:

  • 1 orange, sliced
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 4 cups water
  • Ice cubes

Instructions

  1. Choose your preferred flavor combination from the ingredients list above.
  2. Wash all fruits, vegetables, and herbs thoroughly.
  3. Slice fruits and vegetables thinly for better flavor infusion.
  4. Gently muddle herbs like mint or basil to release their oils (optional).
  5. Add your chosen ingredients to a large pitcher or water bottle.
  6. Pour cold water over the ingredients.
  7. Add ice cubes for immediate serving or chill in refrigerator.
  8. Let the water infuse for at least 30 minutes for mild flavor.
  9. For stronger flavor, refrigerate for 2-4 hours or overnight.
  10. Stir gently before serving.
  11. Serve chilled in glasses with fresh ice.
  12. Refill with water 2-3 times using the same ingredients.

Chef's Tips

  • Use filtered or purified water for the best taste
  • Don't over-muddle herbs as it can make water bitter
  • Change ingredients every 24 hours for food safety
  • Experiment with different fruit and herb combinations
  • Add a pinch of sea salt to enhance flavors naturally
  • Store in refrigerator and consume within 3 days
