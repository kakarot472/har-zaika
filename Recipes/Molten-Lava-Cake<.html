Molten Lava Cake

Prep: 15 min
Cook: 12 min
Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 100g dark chocolate, chopped
  • 100g unsalted butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • Pinch of salt
  • Butter for greasing ramekins
  • Cocoa powder for dusting
  • Vanilla ice cream for serving
  • Powdered sugar for dusting
  • Fresh berries for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425¬∞F (220¬∞C). Grease 4 ramekins with butter and dust with cocoa powder.
  2. Melt chocolate and butter together in a double boiler or microwave, stirring until smooth.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together whole eggs, egg yolks, and sugar until thick and pale.
  4. Slowly add the melted chocolate mixture to the egg mixture, whisking constantly.
  5. Sift in flour and salt, fold gently until just combined. Don't overmix.
  6. Divide batter evenly among prepared ramekins.
  7. Place ramekins on a baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes.
  8. The edges should be firm but centers should still jiggle slightly when shaken.
  9. Remove from oven and let cool for 1 minute.
  10. Run a knife around edges to loosen, then invert onto serving plates.
  11. Tap gently and lift ramekins carefully.
  12. Dust with powdered sugar, add ice cream and berries. Serve immediately.

Chef's Tips

  • Use high-quality dark chocolate for best flavor
  • Don't overbake - centers should be slightly jiggly
  • Grease ramekins well to ensure easy removal
  • Can be prepared ahead and refrigerated, then baked when needed
  • Serve immediately while the center is still molten
  • Perfect timing is key - watch carefully during baking
