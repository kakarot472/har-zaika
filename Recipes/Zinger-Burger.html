Zinger Burger

Prep: 20 min
Cook: 15 min
Serves: 2

Ingredients

For Chicken Fillet:


  • 2 chicken breast fillets
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper

For Coating:


  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
  • Oil for deep frying

For Assembly:


  • 2 burger buns
  • 4 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 2 lettuce leaves
  • 2 tomato slices
  • 2 cheese slices (optional)
  • Pickles (optional)

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken fillets in buttermilk with salt, pepper, garlic powder, paprika, and cayenne for 30 minutes.
  2. Mix all coating ingredients (flour, cornstarch, and spices) in a bowl.
  3. Heat oil to 350¬∞F (175¬∞C) for deep frying.
  4. Remove chicken from marinade and coat thoroughly with the flour mixture.
  5. Dip coated chicken back into buttermilk, then coat again with flour mixture for extra crispiness.
  6. Carefully place chicken in hot oil and fry for 6-8 minutes until golden brown and cooked through.
  7. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  8. Toast burger buns lightly until golden.
  9. Spread mayonnaise on both halves of the bun.
  10. Place lettuce leaf on bottom bun, add fried chicken fillet.
  11. Add cheese slice (if using), tomato slice, and pickles.
  12. Top with the other half of the bun and serve immediately.

Chef's Tips

  • Marinate chicken for at least 30 minutes for maximum flavor
  • Double coating gives extra crispiness to the chicken
  • Maintain oil temperature at 350¬∞F for perfect frying
  • Don't overcrowd the oil when frying
  • Toast buns for better texture and to prevent sogginess
  • Serve immediately while chicken is hot and crispy
